Fellowship Description

The Southwest Research Institute (SwRI) offers Research Assistantships (RA) to qualified graduate students in the joint Ph.D. program between the Physics and Astronomy Department at University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA) and SwRI. Qualified students receive stipend, tuition and benefits to conduct cutting-edge research at SwRI in all areas of Space Physics, Planetary Sciences, and Astrophysics, including instrumentation, data analysis, and modeling. Although the fellowship offered by the joint UTSA/SwRI program adequately covers costs and research needs, students are encouraged to apply to outside fellowships.

External fellowships available include but are not limited to:

Texas Space Grant Consortium Fellowship

NASA Earth and Space Science Fellowship

NASA Harriet Jenkins Fellowship for Women and Minorities

National Science Foundation Graduate Researcher Fellowship

National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate Fellowship
