About us: San Antonio

San Antonio - rated as one of the 52 places to go in 2015 by the NY Times - is home of the historic Alamo (a UNESCO World Heritage site) and the River Walk which intersects museums and other culture sites. Surrounded by the natural beauty of the Texas Hill Country, San Antonio is also only a few hours from the Texas Gulf coast. San Antonio is also only about an hour from Austin, the capital of Texas and the “live music capital of the world”.
