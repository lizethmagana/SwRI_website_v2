Class Schedule

Classes listed are 3 credit hours each. Fall and spring semesters require registering for 9 credit hours to be considered a full-time student, and the summer semester requires 3 credit hours. "Odd" terms indicate classes are available in 2015, 2017, etc., while "Even" indicates availability for 2016, 2018, and so forth.
