Tour Day 2015 Recap

Tour day 2016 was another great success with 35 registered students! Our agenda for the visiting students was:

  • 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM LUNCH AND INTRODUCTIONS
  • 12:45 PM - 2:00 PM LAB TOURS
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM FACULTY PANEL
  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM SCIENCE PRESENTATIONS
  • 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM STUDENT PANEL
  • 5:00 PM ONWARD MEET THE GRAD STUDENTS
We had a great time hosting the prospective students and hope to see them again in the future as part of our program! Tour Day 2016 Flyer

Tour Day 2015 Recap

Tour day 2015 was a great success with 34 registered visitors! Visitors had the chance to listen to information about the program, as well as tour eight of our state of the art labs:

  • Energetic Plasma Instrument Calibration (EPIC) Lab
  • Experimental Space Physics Development Lab
  • High Energy Particle (HEP) Lab
  • Instrument Development and Calibration Lab - Electrons
  • Instrument Development and Calibration Lab - Ions
  • Optics and Detector Lab
  • SwRI Ultraviolet Reflectance Chamber (SwURC)
  • Ultraviolet Radiometric Calibration Chamber
Additionally, visitors got to meet and listen to two presentations from current students!
